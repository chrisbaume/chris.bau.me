Our mission

We believe that innovative audio technology should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their technical know-how or budget. That's why we create easy-to-use and affordable mobile apps that use advanced technology to improve your everyday life.

Our products are based on 15 years of experience in pioneering audio research and user experience design. We use this expertise to design and build products that make cutting-edge audio technology accessible, fun, and delightful to use.

Our story

Founder Chris Baume has worked at the forefront of audio technology research for over 15 years. Chris was a Lead Research Engineer at BBC Research and Development, where he co-led the audio research group. He is interested in how technology can be used to unlock people's creative potential. His research focuses on the efficient production of personalised, immersive, and accessible audio experiences through the application of DSP, machine learning, and user-centred design techniques. He managed the BBC Audio Research Partnership which sponsored over thirty students and launched ten collaborative projects worth £30M. He was co-investigator on the AI for Future Personalised Media Experiences (AI4ME) partnership, where he led the research on production tools. He also led the production tools work stream of the EU project ORPHEUS on object-based audio. He earned his PhD at the Centre for Vision, Speech and Signal Processing (CVSSP) at the University of Surrey.
